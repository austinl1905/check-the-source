What is Misinformation?

Misinformation is an issue that has been intensified by increasing internet usage and decreasing media literacy. It's systematic and employs methods that are effective on impressionable people, especially students from the age of 13 to 18. It has impacts on students both academically and for their well-being. Even if you think you know how to use the internet, stay vigilant, and don’t immediately believe everything you read.

Data

Facts

  • Misinformation actually affects teenagers the most out of any age demographic.

  • Misinformation is most commonly used for promoting a political or social agenda
